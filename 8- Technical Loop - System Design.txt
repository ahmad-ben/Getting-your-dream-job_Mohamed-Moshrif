This type of interview is typically reserved for candidates with  substantial experience. 
  Junior or entry-level candidates rarely encounter it.
Structured approach to tackling system design interviews. 
  It's all about asking the right questions. 
  Framework with three key stages:
    Why -Tactical Group-: 
      Understanding the purpose behind building a system.
      For example:
        Why are we building this.
    What -Strategic Group-: 
      Defining what exactly needs to be built.
    How -Operational Group-: 
      Determining the technical implementation details.
A significant takeaway is that system design is like building with LEGO:
  While the components remain the same, their arrangement defines the final system. 
  There are no "wrong" answers. 
    But there are more efficient and optimized solutions for specific use cases.
